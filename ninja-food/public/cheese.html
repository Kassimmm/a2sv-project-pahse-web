5 Bean Chili Stew with Feta Cheese Ingredients
  • ✓ 8 oz cheese (such as cheddar, mozzarella, or pepper jack), shredded
  • ✓ 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • ✓ 1 tablespoon butter
  • ✓ 1 cup milk
  • ✓ Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions
  • In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
  • Stir in flour and cook for a few minutes until golden brown.
  • Gradually whisk in milk, stirring constantly until thickened.
  • Reduce heat to low and add shredded cheese, stirring until melted and smooth.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Remove from heat and serve immediately as a sauce for pasta, vegetables, or as a dip.
